### Q2. Can I switch umbrella insurance providers mid-year?

In most cases, yes. However, timing matters. Some products have cancellation penalties or waiting periods for new coverage. Plan your switch to avoid coverage gaps and minimize any transition costs.

Financial experts recommend reviewing your umbrella insurance annually or after any major life change (marriage, home purchase, job change, new baby). Market conditions and your personal situation both change over time.
