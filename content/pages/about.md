## Our Mission

Smart Finance Guide helps millions of Americans make smarter financial decisions. We provide free, expert-reviewed guides on insurance, personal finance, loans, and technology tools.

## Editorial Standards

Every article on Smart Finance Guide follows strict editorial standards:

**Accuracy First.** All financial data, rates, and statistics are verified against primary sources including official company rate pages, government databases, and industry reports. We update our content monthly to ensure accuracy.

**Expert Review.** Our content is reviewed by licensed insurance agents, certified financial planners (CFP), and industry professionals before publication.

**No Pay-to-Play.** Our rankings and recommendations are based on objective analysis. Companies cannot pay to influence their placement in our reviews.

**Transparent Methodology.** When we compare products, we clearly explain our testing methodology and scoring criteria so you can evaluate our conclusions.
